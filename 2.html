BORN TO BE BLUE

(Directed by Robert Budreau, 2015)

Born to Be Blueis a 2015 internationally co-produced drama film directed, produced and written by Robert Budreau. The film stars Ethan Hawke and Carmen Ejogo. It was shown in the Special Presentations section of the 2015 Toronto International Film Festival.[7] The film is about American jazz musician Chet Baker, portrayed by Hawke.
